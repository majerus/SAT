This app was designed by Rich Majerus using data from NYC Open Data. Select a variable from the menu to plot each school's value. Hovering over the graph will display labels and values for each of the bars. According to NYC Open Data this data includes the "most recent school level results for New York City on the SAT. Results are available at the school level for the graduating seniors of 2012. Records contain 2012 College-bound seniors mean SAT scores taken during SY 2012." The data is provided in the table and can be searched and sorted to identify schools of interest.
